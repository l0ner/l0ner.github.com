One thing about ubuntu 9.04 that i didnt like when I was using it was blockade of ctrl+alt+backspace, wich normally allow you to restart your desktop without rebooting whole PC. To enable it you can two things: 1). $sudo apt-get install dontzap $sudo dontzap --disable $sudo apt-get remove dontzap 2). $sudo nano /etc/X11/xorg.conf and add this: Section “ServerFlags” Option “DontZap” “false” EndSection I got those solutions form http://404.g-net.pl
