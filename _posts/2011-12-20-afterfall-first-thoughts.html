Just a bunch of my opinions after playing it for 10 minutes.
  1. The camera is too damn close to protagonist
  2. Whole graphics is too fuzzy. Too much of blur effect for me.
  3. Audio: I'm playing english version and the actors sound just unprofessional.
  4. Leaving Polish names in the english version was a really bad idea.
  5. Combat: it's pretty annoying that our character can perform only one move. Besides whole system is too stiff.
  6. Actor play: this is damn unrealistic too. Their movements are just... wrong. Too artificial.
  7. Every text i heard up until now was really stiff. Unnatural.
  8. I like idea of the whole shelter being so big that it needs internal railway. This is something I was expecting from fallout vaults and I've never get.
